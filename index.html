Background Information

I wanted to base my thoughts on a framework that would fit in-line with how Etsy's teams are already operating. Based on my conversation with Randy Hunt back in 2014, I thought it would make sense to use the Problem, Strengths, Weaknesses and Tenets model. I'm going to do a quick analysis on how where Etsy stands from a business perspective, and how that translates into goals for the product team, particularly shop management.
